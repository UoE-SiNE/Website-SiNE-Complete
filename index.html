Technology @SiNE


SiNE is an integration
of various STEM disciplines.

We use Unmanned Vehicular technology and aim to have a closed-loop system using the Swarm Intelligence & Agent Based Modelling algorithms. We intend to design and improve on the latest generation controller systems, to adapt them to our specific custom requirements.
Thus intending on having the hardware more to our preference.


The objective @SiNE is to use cutting edge technology in the fields of sensors, embedded systems, communication, software and decision analytics to build a fully operational Swarm of UV systems.


Using the sensor data our Unmanned Vehicles will analyse the surroundings to complete the tasks assigned. At the same time sharing the acquired data to the swarm network. This collective knowledge keep the Unmanned Vehicles in the network updated at all times of their assigned tasks. Thereby Swarm Intelligence helps in developing a more optimised solution which can change dynamically depending on the situation.


Research @SiNE


The Student Society focuses on
Swarm Intelligence
and its technology applications

We aim to apply the concepts of Swarm Intelligence on our Unmanned Vehicles. Research is the first step of this process. The numerical algorithms in Swarm Intelligence stems from the oberving insects-interactions with itself and its surroundings when in a swarm. Since the past decade behaviour of various swarms are observed. These have helped develop numerical optimisation techniques which have improved functions involving collective knowledge of swarm particles



Multiple Swarm Intelligence based algorithms

These include ant colony optimizer, bee colony algorithm, particle swarm optimzer, glowworm algorithm, firefly algorithm, cuckoo search algorithm, bat algorithm & hunting search algorithm.To kickoff with SiNE, we intend to look at tasks, actions that can be performed using the ant, bee and particle swarm optimization algorithms .
